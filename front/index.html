Do you want to be a part of a Technical and Engineering Emmy® award winning company? Do you want to work with bleeding-edge technology? Okay, then I think this is the position that you are looking for!

ABOUT THE ROLE

As a software developer, you will join a team providing broadcasters and service providers around the globe with world-class products for media transport over any type of network, such as content for major sport events and live TV events. Some of the world's largest media network are built on Nevion's technologies - including AT&T, Arqiva, BBC, BT, NBC Universal, EBU, TDF, HBO, Telenor and NASA. You will be joining the team developing leading management platforms, which is a fully integrated system providing an innovative new approach for management of media networks.

In 2015 Nevion won the Technical and Engineering Emmy® award, and they recently won the Digital Media World award for the best IP based production. Have you heard of the new Stars Wars movie, Solo? Some of the technology used by making the film comes from Nevion...

You will be working mainly with their Management Systems, widely used by several broadcasters to control their video activity. The system has a new and innovative approach to handling media networks, and provides managed services, customer access control, data consolidation across the entire network. In addition to that, it provides a service perspective on broadcast operations. Features like network path finding and in-advance bookings are essential components in the system, and they base their Architecture on recent cloud computing technologies.

JOB DUTIES

You will work in an agile team environment and will be involved in all aspects of development of products; analysis, design, implementation, testing and installation with customers. Nevion are using Scala as a primary programming language, but we would also expect you to be eager when it comes to learning other technologies as well.

As a developer, you will take part in the following activities:

  • All product development phases, from analysis and architectural design to software implementation and testing.
  • Work with the latest audio/video networking and computing technologies.
  • Develop software and solutions in all areas from hardware device communication to audiovisual signal processing and high-level management applications.

You will face technologies like C++, Real-Time systems, IOT, virtualization, computer networks & IP technologies, embedded security, data encryption, GPUs and application containerization.

WE ARE LOOKING FOR

The ideal candidate has a strong interest in programming and wants to learn.Primarily we are search for a good programmer and hope you can relate to some of the following attributes:

  • Enthusiasm about new technology (Show us some of your projects!)
  • Self-motivated and self-directed
  • Teamwork skills
  • Comfortable using C++

You also have a higher degree of education, preferably with grades you are proud of in a relevant field, like mathematics and programming.

OTHER INFORMATION

  • Location: Lysaker, Oslo
  • Start date: As soon as possible, although, we will wait for the right person
  • Information needed when applying: Resume, motivation letter, grades
  • Deadline to apply: July 21

If you have any questions regarding the position or the process, please contact Morten Kristoffersen or Catalina Enes at Academic Work.
